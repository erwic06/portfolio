I've been into mechanical keyboards for a few years now. First one I ever had was a Ducky One Two Mini that my friend gifted to me with Sakura Blossom keycaps. Been addicted ever since (although I don't use Blues' anymore lol)

## My Current Setup

- **Board**: KBD67 Lite R3
- **Switches**: Gateron Milky White (lubed with Krytox 205g0)
- **Keycaps**: GMK Minimal 2
- **Plate**: Poly

## Why I Care

I just love the sound, and its cool customizing something you use everyday.
