# Mechanical Keyboards

I've been into mechanical keyboards for a few years now. There's something deeply satisfying about customizing every aspect of your typing experience.

## My Current Setup

- **Board**: KBD67 Lite R3
- **Switches**: Gateron Oil Kings (lubed with Krytox 205g0)
- **Keycaps**: GMK Minimal 2
- **Plate**: Polycarbonate

## Why I Care

Keyboards are one of the few tools we interact with for hours every day. A well-built keyboard can make the difference between enjoying and enduring your work.

## Resources

If you're interested in getting started, check out community forums and YouTube channels dedicated to the hobby. It's a rabbit hole, but a fun one.
